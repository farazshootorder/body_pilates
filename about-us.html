Fallen in love with dynamic movements from a very young age as a Yoga practitioner and a teacher, she was introduced to Pilates in 2019 in Germany. About Pilates

Popularized by celebrities for its long, lean, and sculpted aesthetic, Pilates is much more than that. Originally called 'Contrology', Pilates is a challenging low impact exercise method that balances strength with mobility, aligns the body, fine-tunes movement patterns, and strengthens the deeper muscles of the core.

Developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 20th century, the Pilates repertoire, which includes mat and specialized equipment exercises, is made up of over 600+ exercises and variations.

Pilates is a whole body exercise system that has numerous benefits, from injury prevention to improved flexibility. Read on to know some of the scientifically backed benefits of Pilates:

  • It increases core strength
  • Improves the metabolism
  • Improves posture
  • Decreases back pain
  • Prevents injuries
  • Increases energy
  • Enhances mind - body connection
  • Improves flexibility and mobility
  • Improves balance
  • Boosts your immunity
  • Improve motivation
  • Improves your sex life
  • Enhances sports performance
  • Strengthens your bones
  • Boosts your mood
  • Improves sleep
  • Encourages playfulness
