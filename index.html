Welcome to BodyScience Pilates

Located in Financial District, Hyderabad, the studio is fully equipped with various Pilates apparatus designed to improve posture, balance and functional movement and run by an internationally recognized Pilates instructor (few fit outs are goings on).

Discover the benefits of a regular Pilates practice with the help of our programs, themes and experience the joy of movement through Pilates and bring ease into your life. Whether you are just a beginner, an advanced practitioner, a Pilates teacher, or looking for classes with specific equipment, you will experience a warm welcome here.

We offer Private, Group Classes, Workshops, Programs and Retreats.
